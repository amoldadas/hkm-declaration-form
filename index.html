Rules and Regulations & Terms & Conditions

  • HKM Pilgrimage Tours are being conducted purely for strengthening and enlivening our spiritual consciousness and to progress in devotional service.
  • Maintain devotional consciousness and not just sight-seeing.
  • Do not preach about your own beliefs/religion/rituals/spirituality which is contrary to the essence of the Vedic Literatures. Such discussions/clarifications are allowed at the end of the lectures/discourses during the Pilgrimage.
  • We do not serve Non-Veg/Egg. We serve Krishna Prasadam only.
  • We do not serve Tea/Coffee.
  • During the entire Pilgrimage every pilgrim should follow – No meat eating including egg, No intoxication, No illicit sex and No gambling.
  • Dress code –Indian Traditional Dress -Men (Kurta-Pajama/Dhoti) Women (Saree, Chudidar-Dupatta). Avoid skirts, minis, shorts & other fancy dresses.

However, do not get into developing mundane love relations/illicit relations/make or propose business with Fellow Pilgrims. Failing which, the tour manager reserves the right to cancel the pilgrimage of the pilgrim without any refund of amount.
  • Lectures and discourses will be in English and Hindi.

  • Any person more than 60 years should be accompanied by a guardian or family member.

Accommodation –

We book standard & decent rooms with twin sharing / maximum of three sharing basis. We may book dormitories at some remote places OR places where it suites the Pilgrimage route plan. Pilgrims have to adjust accordingly. Extra person in a room will be provided with a mattress/bed.

In case you require an extra room for your convenience the same will be arranged at an actual extra cost. Pilgrims, who arrive early OR depart late at the accommodation destination by their own travel arrangements, have to pay extra charges for the days they need the guest house.
Handpicked good tourist hotels all offering private bath are specifically chosen keeping in mind the comforts of the tourist. Some of the hotels may be located in the cities, but to provide with comfortable hotels and reasonable prices, it is often necessary to use hotels away from city centers. In some cases, the hotels/accommodation may not have all amenities like hot water, extra beds, etc. Participants have to adjust with the accommodation and not demand the tour manager for better accommodation.
